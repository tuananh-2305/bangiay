Guide to measuring shoe size for men and women

Step 1. Prepare the following items to measure your foot size:

  • A piece of paper (bigger than a foot)
  • A ruler
  • A pen (write)
  • What socks/socks do you plan to wear with the shoes you need to buy?

Step 2. Steps to measure foot size:

After going all the way, you stamp your foot hard on the paper and keep it firmly so that the foot does not change the size after the measurement will be wrong. Then, use the pen to trace the frame of your foot onto the paper.

Step 3. Measure the foot length:

Mark 4 points including 2 tips of the toes and 2 ends of the heel with a pen. Then use a ruler to measure the length of these two points.

Step 4. Measure the width of the foot:

In addition to the length, then you need to measure the width of the foot. You just need to use a ruler or measuring tape, then choose the position closest to the top of the toe with the width or the longest instep (you see where the widest part of the foot is) to measure, when you measure you should be in a sitting or standing position with both legs straight.

Step 5. Calculate the result to choose the shoe size:

Sau khi đo xong bàn chân, bạn hãy lấy chiều dài nhất của chân và chiều rộng nhất và tính theo công thức sau:

Shoe size = N = L (foot length) + 1.5cm

After you have the results, you can use them to compare according to the shoe size conversion table below. Surely with this way, you will choose the most suitable shoes for your foot size.

For example: You measure the length of your foot is 22.5cm, then your shoe size will add another 1.5cm, the result is 24cm. Then you compare with the cm column in the shoe size conversion table below to find out your shoe size is 38 - 39 for Vietnamese size, UK size is 6, and US size is 8.

Note when measuring shoes?

  • You should measure when your feet are in the most comfortable condition, or measure at night to get the most accurate size.
  • Please measure both feet, because our feet will not be equal, so you will take the size of which foot is longer and wider.

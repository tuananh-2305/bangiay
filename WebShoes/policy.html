Policy

Tanh shop always strives to bring a great shopping experience to customers from diversifying models from many famous international brands, online and offline sales consulting services and non-stop after-sales services. complete.

1. Product warranty policy

Warranty conditions

Tanh shop supports customers with 3 months warranty for free

  1. Products must be distributed by Tanh shop.
  2. The product is still within the warranty period and is damaged due to the manufacturer's technical errors: open glue, peeling sole, broken elastic, broken thread.
  3. Customers must present a valid product warranty card or have complete purchase information on the system.

Tanh shop refuses to provide product warranty in the following cases:

  1. No purchase invoice information
  2. Damaged products and errors caused by customers such as scratches, worn soles, products are not intact due to animal bites, improper storage causes mold, fading, melting.
After the warranty period expires, the shop still supports shoe repair at a reasonable cost at reputable repair facilities for customers during use.

Warranty period

Handling and returning products that have been repaired and warranted to customers within a period of 05 working days from the date of receipt of the product (except for special situations or having to find difficult-to-replace materials, the Shop will contact and negotiate directly with customers).

3. Return Policy

Tanh shop's return policy was born with the desire to give customers peace of mind the best shopping experience. Products of Thanh Hung Futsal, when sent to customers, are always guaranteed to be new, high-quality goods, in accordance with the description and pictures on the website.

What should the customers change products?

Customers are allowed to exchange products for another product when they receive a product that does not match the description on the website, the product is faulty by the manufacturer, does not fit the size or simply is no longer satisfied with the purchased product.

Refund policy of Tanh shop

Customers can change to another product only once and within 7 days from the time of receiving the goods. Exchange products must meet the following conditions:

  • Product has not been used, not dirty. (When trying on footwear products, it is recommended that you wear socks and test the shoes on a clean floor to ensure that the product is not soiled.)
  • In case the product to be exchanged is ordered during the promotion period, please exchange the product during the promotion period to be applied the preferential price.

How to return the product?

Customers who need to exchange products can do it in 1 of 2 ways:

  • Option 1: Exchange directly at the store: Please bring the product with the invoice and the warranty card (or warranty card) to one of the Tanh stores for assistance in exchanging the product
  • Option 2: Change by post: Please send products, accompanying accessories, promotional gifts (if any), enclosed with invoices (if any) to the following address: 277A/3 Viet Nam

After receiving the product, Tanh will conduct product inspection according to product exchange regulations (this process takes about 2-3 days). Tanh will notify the customer by phone after the test is completed.

About exchange cost

  • Free exchange for customers when shopping at Thanh Hung Futsal in case the product has a manufacturing defect, is scratched in transit, or is delivered by mistake.
  • However, in case the customer wants to change the product for subjective reasons such as the wrong size, dislike the color..., please pay the 2-way shipping fee.

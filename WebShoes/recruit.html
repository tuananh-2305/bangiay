Sales Consultant (Full/Part-time)

Published date: April 6, 2022.

WAGE AGREEMENT.

Age between 18 and 30 years old.

JOB DESCRIPTION:

  • Mastering product knowledge, being responsible for consulting and introducing products and goods to customers.
  • Follow all store rules.
  • Implement and maintain product display activities in the store.
  • Handle stock quickly and efficiently, constantly replenishing products to ensure all store products are displayed in the sales area.
  • Complete sales transactions quickly and accurately.
  • Minimize loss of goods in the warehouse and in the sales area.
  • Perform other duties as required by management.

FLEXIBLE CASH ROLLING:

  • Morning shift: 08:00 - 13:00.
  • Lunch shift: 13:00 - 17:00.
  • Afternoon shift: 17:00 - 21:00 (One day off a week from Monday to Friday).
  • Full time: 9am - 6pm.

REQUEST:

  • Responsibility is an important factor, responsibility at work, responsibility to customers, responsibility to colleagues..
  • Agility, honesty, good communication skills.
  • Sales experience is an advantage.
  • Interest in retail, fashion, sports.

SKILLS REQUIRED:

  • Basic excel skills, proficient in computer handling.
  • Good communication skill.
  • Basic English.

BENEFIT:

  • Full social insurance for Full-Time employees.
  • Attractive salary, bonus based on monthly sales
  • 13th month salary.
  • Incentives for employees.
  • Food allowance 35,000 VND / 8 hours.
